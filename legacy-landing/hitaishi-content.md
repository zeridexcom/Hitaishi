## FOR INSTITUTIONS

**Headline:** Your students deserve more than what a classroom alone can give.

**Intro:** Hitaishi partners with JEE coaching institutes and colleges to embed structured 1-on-1 mentorship directly into your program — without touching your timetable, without extra burden on your faculty, and without disrupting what already works.

**Offering cards:**

1. **Curriculum-Integrated Mentorship Program** — We don't operate parallel to your institution — we integrate into it. Hitaishi mentorship sessions are mapped to your academic calendar, scheduled around your timetable, and aligned with what your students are currently studying. Seamless by design.
2. **Dedicated IITian & Top-Ranker Mentors** — Every student in your institution gets access to a vetted IITian or JEE top-ranker as their personal mentor. This is the kind of edge your students couldn't get elsewhere — and now it's part of what you offer them.
3. **Hostel & Residential Program Ready** — Running a residential coaching or college hostel? Hitaishi is built for it. All sessions are online and flexible, designed to slot into evening free periods or weekend windows — without violating curfews or conflicting with study hours.
4. **Student Progress Reporting** — As an institution, you get regular visibility into how your students are progressing — engagement levels, weak areas being addressed, mentor feedback summaries. Data that helps you make better decisions about your students' futures.
5. **Zero Faculty Burden** — Your teachers teach. Hitaishi mentors guide. There's no overlap, no extra work pushed onto your staff. We are a complete, self-sufficient mentorship layer — managed entirely by us on your behalf.
6. **Flexible Partnership Models** — Whether you want to offer Hitaishi as an add-on service, integrate it into your fee structure, or run it as a pilot batch — we work with your model, not against it. Let's design a partnership that works for both sides.
